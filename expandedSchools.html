About

ExpandED Schools is an education nonprofit serving students in the broader New York City metro area. I currently work as Program Director of Tutoring Policy and Sustainability on the High-Impact Tutoring team. High-Impact Tutoring is a research backed academic intervention method that gained national recognition following school closures during the covid-19 pandemic. As a part of this team, I work to implement and sustain this intervention method in partership with New York City Public Schools.
